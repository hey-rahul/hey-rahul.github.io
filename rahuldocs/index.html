Know which exams you actually need

3 major factors which decide the exams you need to prepare for studying abroad from India are

  1. Field of study
  2. College/University of study
  3. Chosen country

So confirm the admission details for the colleges you are targeting and check which exam you should be taking to be eligible for the admission process.
